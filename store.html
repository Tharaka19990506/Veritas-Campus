Veritas International (Pvt) Ltd – Colombo Harbor Duty-Free Shop
Your Trusted Maritime Partner in Colombo Harbor.

Located within the heart of Sri Lanka’s busiest and most strategic port, Veritas International (Pvt) Ltd operates a fully equipped duty-free shop dedicated to serving vessels, tug crews, and all maritime personnel calling at Colombo Harbor. As an essential arm of the harbor’s logistics and service ecosystem, we specialize in providing a wide range of duty-free goods, including:

  • Branded liquors and tobacco products
  • Confectionery and snacks
  • Personal care and hygiene products
  • Household items and general provisions
  • Specialty products tailored to maritime needs

Our facility is designed to offer efficiency, accessibility, and compliance, ensuring that all services meet international maritime standards. With a focus on quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction, Veritas International is proud to support the global shipping community, offering seamless shopping experiences and timely supply solutions right at the port.

Whether you’re part of a cargo crew, tug service, or a visiting vessel, Veritas is your go-to destination for trusted duty-free services at Colombo Harbor.
